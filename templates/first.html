Welcome To Our Virtual Memory App

Are you forgetting Something??
Don't worry. Our this WebApp helps you to remember everything.

Simple UI

Following on from quick capture, overall the app should be simple. Simple to use. Simple user interface.

Edit And Delete

You can edit and delete your work at any time just with a single click. Both options are provided with the work.

Today List

And one of the best ways to “get work done” is to only concentrate on your top tasks for today.

Future Tasks

Helps you to manage your future tasks easily. Just add them to your list at desirable Date and Time.

Pretty Design

I bet you didn’t expect this one.This is basic UI design. The app has to look good and be readable, too.

Color Coding

Different Colors for the work. For every work there is a color which depends upon its Date and Time.

How to use our To-Do App

STEP 1

To use our this webapp, you need an account. So click on SignUp Button. If you already have an account, Goto Step 2.

STEP 2

Using your Username and password login into your account to use our services. It will redirect you to our HomePage.

STEP 3

At HomePage you will see an Add Work button in Navbar. Use this button to create your To-Do works.

STEP 4

You can Edit your work by clicking on the Edit button. When the task is done, you can Delete it.

STEP 5

According to your scheduled time of work, rows of table change their respective Color. Now Enjoy our App.
